About:
Aside from web development I have a second burning passion: helping people. I came up with the idea for this site when trying to decide how I could combine my two favorite passions! Support My Mission allows users to raise money for missions trips by creating projects and receiving donations. This site includes a CMS for users to create and edit their missions.
